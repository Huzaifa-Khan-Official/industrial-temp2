About us

We are one of the largest home textile exporters, and leading suppliers to top importers & retailers, providing the complete home solutions to the end consumers

Our Story

Kam International is a part of Mekotex Group of Companies and was established in Karachi, Pakistan in the year of 1979.

Our strong and consistent history of perfection make us a proud company today. We are one of the largest home textile exporters from Pakistan, and leading supplier to top importers & retailers, by providing the complete home solutions to the end consumers.

We are continuously evolving our progress, process of expansion, and improving ourselves with the help of latest technologies & innovative ideas in order to accommodate our customers in the best possible way. Kam International has a highly qualified management team and professional technical staff with a standardized working system.

Furthermore, Kam International has a highly integrated network of ginning, spinning, weaving, printing, dyeing, finishing, cutting & stitching units under their own setup.

At present, Kam International supplies directly to majority of private and famous retail brands of UK, USA and Europe. Our annual sales count of export deliveries exceeds to 99.9 percent of entire and productions. The USD Annual Turnover is 250 million as a whole group, and home textile group volume is USD 65-70 million. We are a vertically integrated company and top leading exporter of home textile and accessories in Karachi, Pakistan.

History

In the early 1971, Mekotex (Pvt).Ltd. started from a small set-up as a fabric trading in Textile industry. Over a very short period of time, we founded our own processing unit for dyeing/printing in the year of 1979; with our countless efforts and seamless progress, we established our very first spinning unit in 1990. International demand of home textile led to the creation Kam International in the year of 1992. Alongside growth and expansion Mekotex and Kam International needed a weaving unit. In 1996 we put up an operation of Dobby looms/Jacqurd Looms/Sulzer looms and Air-jet looms. In the year of 1999, Mekotex established its name in Denim and in a very short period of time become one of the biggest producers, sellers, and exporters of Denim fabrications. 2007, was the year of up-gradation of plant and equipment to compete the international market in processing and weaving. Keeping up with the expectations of our customers, a huge investment in latest digital printing technology was made in 2016. Kam International now has the biggest capacity of Digital Printing in Pakistan. In present, a complete up-gradation of the whole processing plant (completion date February 2020). Latest wider width high speed Digital Printing Machine added (operational date end January 2020). In addition of 150 Air-jet Waving Machines (tentative completion date June 2020). A complete new narrow width continuous dyeing plant (completion date end 2020).
